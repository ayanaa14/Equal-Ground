About

“I started this website because I often felt that important issues in the world were either not discussed enough or were addressed in ways that didn’t feel genuine.

Feminism, politics, and global events are not distant topics, they affect people's daily lives every single day. Women are constantly pushed back, urged to remain in the shadows, and are often told to be seen and not heard. No one should be forced to accept this ideology. We must speak out not only for ourselves but also for the women who cannot.

This is a free space of no judgement, for our youth to write their hearts out for the world to finally see them as not just children, but hope. Hope that you will create a better and safer world.”

Who Are We?
